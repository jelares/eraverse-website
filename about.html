What is the Eraverse?

A platform for deeper online interaction with the people you love and the world around you. Eraverse is a social layer built on top of the internet, with financial assets, in the form of tokens, woven into the inner workings of everything you do. It is a group of embodied online social services linked together by an underlying social and economic data structure on the blockchain, all connected by just a single account.

Who's building it?

We are a team of MIT students passionate about changing the world.
