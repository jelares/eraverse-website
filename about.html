What is the Eraverse?

A platform for deeper online interaction with the people you love and the world around you. It is a vision for an internet with a sense of space built-in, with financial assets, in the form of tokens, into the inner workings of almost anything you do online.

Who's building it?

We are a team of MIT students passionate about changing the world.
